Wild Jobi

About this work

Through a hybrid photographic workflow, Wild Jobi investigates the psychological landscapes of the "in-between." His work utilizes both traditional analog formats and modern digital tools to capture the atmospheric qualities of transition. This interplay of mediums reflects a deep interest in the concepts of object relations and psychological boundaries.
